**Prompt:** Write a complete story that fits in a single paragraph.

The yellowed paper revealed coordinates leading to a hidden beachside cottage where her grandmother had once lived as a young woman. With trembling hands, Lila followed the map, her heart aching with grief and curiosity. At the cottage, she found a shoebox filled with photographs and love letters from a man named Thomas, whom her grandmother never spoke of. Lila wept beneath the starlit sky, piecing together fragments of a past that had shaped her family’s silent whispers.
